Outside work, I'm on fur baby duty, conquering trails, sipping at new coffee spots, and soaking in family vibes. Life's an adventure!

Lucky Charm

Meet the one and only Lucky, my adorable golden retriever! She's not just a fluffy bundle of cuteness; she's a dynamic burst of energy. With more than 30 toys at her disposal, you'd think she'd be occupied, but nope—she'd rather engage in a game of nibble-the-fingers and toes. Despite the occasional nibbling, she's proving to be an exceptional pup, quickly mastering commands at just a few months old. It's a thrilling journey shaping her into the ultimate "goodest girl" on the block!

Garden of the Gods

Beyond my passion for jazzing up websites and making them as amazing and user-friendly as possible, my heart belongs to the great outdoors. There's something magical about being surrounded by towering trees, inhaling that crisp, fresh air, and catching glimpses of incredible wildlife. Ever laid eyes on a grizzly in the wild? It's a wild experience, let me tell you! The photo I've got here is from a hike in Colorado's Garden of the Gods—an absolute gem with breathtaking views and a crowd to match. I'm itching to revisit in winter, envisioning the landscape draped in snow. Though, I might skip the hike then; no need to dance with danger in freezing temperatures!

Family Times

I'm the baby of the family, and there's nothing I cherish more than our growing bunch. Last July, I had the joy of jetting off to Colorado to hang with my brother and his clan. We ticked off all the boxes of my favorite activities—exploring new breweries, tackling hikes (thanks, elevation), marveling at epic lightning storms, and even scoring a solo adventure to a mind-blowing concert in Denver. Let's just say, I'm seriously considering a repeat performance or maybe even plotting a relocation. Colorado's got a piece of my heart now!
